1. DAMAGE AND LOSS RESPONSIBILITY

Renter is responsible for any and all damages and losses to the rented equipment. For purposes of calculation of damages or loss, the Replacement Value of any equipment shall be defined as 80% of MSRP (Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price).

Cost of repair, retrieval, or rescue of the rented equipment will be charged at:

  • $75 per man-hour, plus
  • $0.50 per mile for any required travel
  • Common repair/replacement costs (examples: hull breach $200, lost PFD $60, lost paddle $50, lost straps $20)

The security deposit will be applied to any damages. If damages exceed the deposit amount, Renter agrees to pay the balance within 14 days of receiving an itemized invoice from Owner.

3. CANCELLATION POLICY

In case the Renter needs to cancel a rental, or in case of a no-show at the agreed time and place, the Owner will reserve the right to keep the down payment paid when making the reservation. The Renter has no right to any form of reimbursement.

In the event of cancellation, the following charges apply:

Cancellation Timing Refund Amount
More than 15 days before rental start 80% refund (20% cancellation fee)
7-14 days before rental start 50% refund (50% cancellation fee)
Less than 7 days before rental start 20% refund (80% cancellation fee)
No-show or same-day cancellation No refund (100% charged)

Security deposits are fully refundable regardless of cancellation timing, provided no damage has occurred.

4. LATE RETURN POLICY

Timely return is critical to meet commitments to other renters. Late fees may accrue after a 15-minute grace period, at a rate of 10% of the 24-hour rate for each item per full or part of each hour beyond the originally scheduled return deadline.

After 4 hours late, a full additional day's rental will be charged.

If Renter needs to extend the rental period, they must contact Owner at least 24 hours in advance to request an extension. Extensions are subject to availability and Owner approval.
